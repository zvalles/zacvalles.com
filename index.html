I studied aerospace engineering at UMichigan and spent some time there researching fluid mechanics to build supercavitating hydrofoils and torpedos. Before that, I studied mechanical engineering at UCR where a few friends and myself developed one of the first AWD electric vehicle's in the North American Formula SAE competition.

Currently I work at Tesla on the powertrain engineering team. I am working on future programs castings similar to this and drive unit bushings similar to this. I love to build. From consumer products that emphasize design vision and consumer experience to solving hard physics problems that take are combined heat transfer, material science, and structural mechanics challenges, I love to build.
