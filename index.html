I studied aerospace engineering at UMichigan and spent some time there researching fluid mechanics to build supercavitating hydrofoils and torpedos.

Now I work at Tesla working on mechanical systems on Robotaxi, mostly the systems that move the vehicle. I like building electromechanical systems that make things move. I also like things that use energy dense atoms to move objects through a vacuum or to provide power for society.

I made this website to document my career, personal projects, and writings about life which you'll find below.
